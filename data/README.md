ZVV GTFS feed (tram, bus, and local rail schedules).

Source: https://opendata.swiss/en/dataset/zvv-fahrplan-tram-und-bus-static-gtfs1/resource/c912506b-a84c-45f3-8155-48f2a568bfc7

Used to extract S18 (Forchbahn) and S17 (Limmattal) station data that was missing from the OpenStreetMap Overpass query. The GTFS feed does not include most S-Bahn lines (S2-S42) — those are in SBB's feed, and were already captured via the Overpass API.
